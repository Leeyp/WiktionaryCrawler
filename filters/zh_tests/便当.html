Mandarin

simpl. 便当
trad. 便當

Etymology 1

  • 便 easy; convenient + 当 to be regarded as

Adjective

便当 (simplified, Pinyin biàndang, traditional 便當)

  1. convenient, easy

Etymology 2

  • From Japanese: 弁当 bento

Noun

便当 (simplified, Pinyin biàndāng, traditional 便當)

  1. (Taiwan) bento
Synonyms
  • (Standard Mandarin) 饭盒 meal box
  • (Standard Mandarin) 盒饭 box meal
  • (Standard Mandarin) 方便 convenient
