Mandarin

Etymology

An abbreviation of the Min Nan word lāu-khok-khok (老柝柝)

Initialism

LKK

  1. (Taiwan) old and senile
  2. (Taiwan) an old geezer; geriatric
    • 2002: 陶禮君 (Tao Lijun), 燈光美氣氛佳 inhouse (Liberty times lifestyle section: Great lighting and a good atmosphere -- inhouse)
      音樂，是inhouse讓人流連忘返的主元素，這裡沒有LKK的搖滾或SPP的Disco，而是播放帶著Chillout曲風的Jazz、R&B、RAP或是NEW AGE。 (t)
      音乐，是inhouse让人流连忘返的主元素，这里没有LKK的摇滚或SPP的Disco，而是播放带着Chillout曲风的Jazz、R&B、RAP或是NEW AGE。 (s)
      Music is the main element to inhouse that makes you lose all track of time and not want to leave. There is no geriatric rock or cheesy disco here. Instead, they play Jazz, R&B, RAP or New Age songs that have a chillout vibe to them.
