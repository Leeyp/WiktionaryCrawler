Mandarin

simpl. 几个
trad. 幾個

Determiner

几个 (几个) (jǐgè or jǐge)

  1. a few; several; some
    你可不可以推荐几个比较好的学校？
    nǐ kě bǔ kěyǐ tuījiàn jǐgè bǐjiǎo hǎo de xuéxiào?
    Could you recommend a few good schools?

Adverb

几个 (simplified, Pinyin jǐgè or jǐge, traditional 幾個)

  1. how many
    宝宝几个月会爬？
    bǎobǎo jǐgè yuè huì pá?
    At how many months can a baby crawl?

Usage notes

  • Technically, 几个 jǐgè should only be used with nouns that have 个 gè as a primary measure word. Moreoever, 几个 jǐgè is only used when the answer to "how many?" is expected to be under ten. For amounts that are expected to exceed ten, 多少 duōshǎo is used.
