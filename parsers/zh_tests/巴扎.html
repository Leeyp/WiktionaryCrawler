Mandarin[edit]

simpl. 巴扎
trad. 巴紮

Etymology[edit]

  • From Uyghur بازار (bazar), from Persian بازار (bâzâr).

Noun[edit]

  • 巴扎 (simplified, Pinyin bāzhā, traditional 巴紮)
  1. (dialectal, Xinjiang) market

Related terms[edit]

  • 巴刹 (pa1sat1)

Usage notes[edit]

  • Dialect, use only by the peoples in Xinjiang U. A. R., China.
