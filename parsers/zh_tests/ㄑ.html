Translingual[edit]

Han character[edit]

archaic character ㄑ quǎn, graphic root of the character 巛 chuān (modern 川)

Letter[edit]

ㄑ

  1. The 13th letter of the Zhuyin fuhao alphabet, followed by ㄒ, and representing /q/.
